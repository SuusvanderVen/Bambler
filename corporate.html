Justification document  

Marketing

Context of campaign and promotional activities

The media and communication plan we have created is about advertising our brand on a social media platform . The most used social media platform is Facebook. But after doing more research we found that younger people till the age of 30 are most likely to watch their content on Instagram and TikTok. Therefore, the platform we have chosen is Instagram because on Instagram you can make a business account and you can post every content you want. From photos to videos. Our target group are students from age 18-25 and after doing research we have concluded that Instagram is the best way to connect with them and to achieve our objectives. We wanted to post three times a week an informative photo and two times a week a story to keep them interested in our brand. Also, if you show that you are active, more people will follow you. On Instagram you can see statistics from each post and each account so that we can easily reflect on our objectives. We have created multiple objectives :

Marketing communication objective:

For our marketing objective we want to improve brand awareness by launching our brand in the fashion industry and be within the 130.000-150.000 on the world raking list before December 31st 2022.

Communication objective:

For our communication objective, we want to create awareness for our brand ‘’Bambler’’ by reaching 60 people on our social media platforms before the 30th of April 2022. We want to achieve this objective by posting multiple times on our Instagram page, whilst using hashtags that attract people that are interested in this kind of brand.

Reach objective:

For our reach objective we want to reach in total 60 people with the usage of our social media’s. We want to achieve this objective by posting our social media content on the times our target audience is most active on Instagram. In our interviews we have conducted, this is after dinner, so between the hours of 19:00-00:00.

Emotional objective:

For our emotional objective we want to have in total 50 likes in total on our social media platforms. We want to achieve this objective by speaking to our customer/visitor. Our posts will be informational and are based on the emotional needs of our customers/visitors.

Behavioural objective:

For our behavioural objective we want our customers to visit our Instagram page 50 times in total. We want to achieve this objective by posting Instagram-stories, as these lead our customers/visitors to our Instagram page.

Learning Points

If we look back on the objectives we have created at the beginning of this project, we can conclude that we did a good job with reaching the target audience. Our communication and reach goal were to reach 60 people on our social media account before the 30th of April. We have reached over 85 people before the 14th. We probably reached this because we posted consistently. Our emotional objective was to receive 50 likes in total on our post. We got a total likes of 76 on our posts. Our frequency objective was to interest people with the use of advertisement, although we did not use any advertising on Instagram. Instagram has some good options to advertise your brand and it gives you a lot more statistics. This is something we could have looked deeper into. Another learning point is the time we posted. After the problem interviews, we have planned a day and time to post our content. However sometimes we were too late and that resulted in less interaction with the target group. We noticed in the statistics that less people started to follow us after we posted too late. We have also made stories on Instagram but from our 44 followers only 10 people watched those. A learning point for us could be to find something that more people will watch our content on stories.

Future Planning

For each post we made an idea and a message to put this in our planning. You can find the planning with the link underneath this page. We have made the layout by using a media planner app which showed us how our Instagram feed will look like. By using this app our feed is coherent, which is important to our target audience. The planning went till the deadline of this project, however we have planned into the future. In the planning you can see our ideas. In the future we want to involve our visitors more so that people can engage with our webpage. If people engage more with our posts, they see a lot more of our content because of the algorithm. We can involve our target group more by making quizzes, so they have to react on our stories of our posts. We have thought of a quiz about how sustainable you are so that you can reflect that on yourself. We also wanted to make a post about the ins and outs of cotton and bamboo so that our followers clearly can see the differences. That shows them why we are making sweaters out of bamboo.

Furthermore, our reflection on our work, what we would do differently based on our experience in this project related to the marketing assignment. We all agreed upon one thing: start earlier. We think we overestimated the amount of work that needed to be done. In our defence, the constant changes of the project brief, where to deliver did not help us in this journey. In light of posting marketing content on the Instagram page, we did well. We tried different algorithms to see when we would get the most likes and comments, we posted as the content calendar said and this worked well. All in all, it is a very big assignment to just do in 6 weeks with 3 people. It took a hard toll on us, mentally and physically.

About us

Bambler 

Vision

The vision of Bambler is all about being sunstainable and to cherish the world we live on. Therfore, the vision statement of Bambler is:

''In a world of drastically changing environments, we believe that people should be empowerd to start altering the world by living more sustainably because only then we can keep living on the earth we cherish.''

Values

Bambler has created 4 values at which we want to focus on. Let's sum them up:

-Trust, as a brand we want our customers to trust us. We want our customers to trust us for being sustainable and reliable. Trust is one of the most important brand values a brand has.             

- Creativity, Bambler is not just your regular sustainable brand. We want to give our customers products they can choose from. Everyone has their own style. Wheter it is calm and collective or a print all over the place, we've got you!      

- Comfort, at Bambler, being comfortable is important at all times. That is why our products are light weigthed and thermo. The bamboo fabric is soft and feels nice to the skin. There are no annoying tags into the clothing, so no itch! 

- Disclosure, Bambler is openly about the way we work and go in the industry. We show this in our posts and stories at the Instagram page.
